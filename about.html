How it works

Gesture Capture: MPU6050 reads motion and sends to ESP32.

Processing: Filtering and calibration give accurate roll/pitch/yaw.

Transmission: BLE sends data to receiver ESP32.

Control: Receiver maps to servo angles.

Puppet Movement: Seven servos move joints.

Storytelling: Puppet narrates ancient stories.
